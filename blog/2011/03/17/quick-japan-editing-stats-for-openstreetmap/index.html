Quick Japan Editing Stats for OpenStreetMap

Mar 17th, 2011

I was putting together some really quick editing stats about Japan post earthquake/tsunami.  I realized I should probably share them with the larger community rather than keep them to myself.

218 different individuals have editing in Japan in OpenStreetMap.  Those editors have edited 389,431 nodes (this includes adding/removing/changing) .  This has all happened through 1,409 editing sessions.

Keep it up everyone!
