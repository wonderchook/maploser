Mapping of the Recovery

Feb 18th, 2009

While visiting Recovery.gov I noticed there was a thematic map displaying Estimated Job Creation. The Estimated Job Effect map looks like a fairly innocuous map. Until I point out that the U.S. Government has outsourced this map to India. I find it somewhat ironic that the U.S. government has outsourced the display of estimated job growth in the U.S. due to the stimulus. Perhaps there should be a tiny map of India added to account for the $499 roughly that would have been spent on the licensing of Fusion Maps.
